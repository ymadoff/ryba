## Logs

Archive the logs generated by Nagios.

      @backup
        header: 'Logs'
        name: 'logs'
        source: nagios.log_dir
        filter: "nagios.log*"
        target: nagios.backup_dir
        interval: month: 1
        retention: count: 2

## Configuration

Backup the active Nagios configuration.

      @execute
        header: 'Configuration'
        name: 'conf'
        source: '/etc/nagios'
        target: nagios.backup_dir
        interval: month: 1
        retention: count: 2
